Essence of Feelings

Essence of feelings is a truly global brand and household name. Established as a clothing fashion brand, EOF is now also an expert in a range of accessories. Since 1994 and the launch of Perfumes, EOF also produce a wonderful range of premium designer fragrances for both men and women. Famous for its all-American preppy style, the brand was created in 1994 and still remains internationally successful.
